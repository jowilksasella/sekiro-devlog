Combat Mechanics

3.1 Posture & Stun

Attacks deal Posture Damage. When Posture reaches max, the character enters a "Stunned" state, leaving them open to a fatal blow.

Event: OnPostureBroken
Action: Play StunMontage

3.2 The Deflection Window

Precision is key. The SekiroDeflectComponent measures timing:

  • < 0.2s: Perfect Parry Sparks
  • > 0.2s: Guard Safe
  • None: Direct Hit Damage

3.3 Deathblow (處決)

When an enemy is Stunned, the player can perform a "Deathblow". The combat component triggers the ExecutionMontage on the attacker and DeathMontage on the victim.
